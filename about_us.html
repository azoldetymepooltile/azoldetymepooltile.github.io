About Us

Arizona Olde Tyme Pool Services has been cleaning pool tiles for years. We provide old fashioned service and use modern, eco-friendly technology to remove scaling and calcium on your pool tiles and water features without harsh chemicals or the tedious and damaging effects of other tools or processes. So if you need pool tile calcium removal, give us a call!

Trustworthy
