About Us

Arizona Olde Tyme Pool Tile Services has been cleaning pool tiles, fountains, and water features since 2009. We provide old fashioned service with modern, eco-friendly technology to remove scaling and calcium from your pool tiles. We do not use harsh chemicals or other tools or processes which may damage your tile. If you need pool tile calcium removal, give us a call!

Trustworthy

Arizona Olde Tyme Pool Tile Service is trusted by many of the top rated hotels and resorts in the Tucson area to keep their pools looking good, let us help you as well!
